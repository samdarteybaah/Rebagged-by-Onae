Who We Are

ReBagged by Onaé is a Ghanaian brand dedicated to creating stylish, foldable, and eco‑friendly reusable bags designed to combat plastic waste. Our bags are made with high‑quality, sustainable materials to ensure durability and contribute to a greener planet. We believe in promoting conscious consumption and empowering individuals to make a positive environmental impact while staying fashionable.

What Sustainable Fashion Can Be

Why We Do It

Our mission is to offer a sustainable alternative to plastic bags, one of the largest contributors to environmental pollution. Ghana produces over 840,000 tonnes of plastic waste annually, with only 9.5% recycled. As the nation grapples with these challenges, we aim to lead by example by providing practical and stylish alternatives that encourage eco‑conscious behaviour.
